Lasagna

This is a recipe for my mom's famous lasagna.

It's a guaranteed crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

You will need:

  • 1 can marinara sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 bag shredded mozzarella
  • 1 container cottage cheese
  • 9 lasagna noodles

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Cook meat until fully browned
  3. Mix sauce, meat, and cheeses together
  4. Layer three noodles, followed by mixed ingredients
    • Repeat 3 times
  5. Bake for 25 minutes
